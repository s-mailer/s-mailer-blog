## What is S-Mailer?

**S-Mailer** is a multichannel messaging platform for businesses. Send SMS,
WhatsApp, and email to your customers from one simple, unified API — or straight
from the dashboard.

### Why S-Mailer

- **One API, every channel.** Reach customers wherever they are without juggling
  multiple services.
- **Flexible sending.** Deliver through your own devices or high-reliability
  connections, whichever fits your volume and budget.
- **Transparent billing.** Top up tokens and pay per message — no surprises.
- **Built for Mozambique.** Local support and payment options, made by
  [Smartek](https://smartek.co.mz)

### How it works

1. **Top up tokens.** Billing is transparent and per message.
2. **Connect a sender.** Set up a sending method in a few clicks.
3. **Send.** Use the API or the dashboard, and track delivery in real time.
